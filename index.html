How It Works

Three steps to your
perfect appraisal

From initial site visit to final delivery, every TKM appraisal follows a meticulous, data-driven process refined over years of professional practice in the Treasure Valley market.

01

Gather Info

An on-site observation is performed — the dwelling is measured with laser precision, every room photographed, and detailed notes made about finishes and updates. A complete picture is formed before a single number is run.

02

Create Report

Multiple forms of rigorous analysis are applied — paired sales, grouped data, sensitivity, and regression analysis. Comparing your property to recently sold dwellings produces a defensible, accurate opinion of value.

03

Delivery

Your completed appraisal PDF is delivered directly via email. Questions don't stop at delivery — TKM is always available to explain the methodology and walk you through every conclusion reached.

How It Works

Three steps to your
perfect appraisal

From the initial site visit to final delivery, every appraisal follows a thorough, data-driven process built on years of professional practice in the Treasure Valley market.

01

Gather Info

An on-site inspection is performed where the dwelling is measured, every room is photographed, and detailed notes are made about the condition, finishes, and any updates. A thorough picture of the property is documented before any analysis begins.

02

Create Report

Multiple forms of analysis are applied including paired sales, grouped data, sensitivity, and regression analysis. The subject property is compared to recent sales in the area and adjustments are made to arrive at a well-supported, accurate opinion of value.

03

Delivery

The completed appraisal report is delivered as a PDF directly to the client via email. After delivery, Todd is always available to answer questions and walk through the methodology used to arrive at the opinion of value.

Every assignment gets the same level of attention regardless of price range or complexity. The work speaks for itself in the accuracy and quality of every report delivered.

Our Story

Todd Myers founded TKM Appraisals to bring accurate, data-driven appraisals to the Treasure Valley. The real estate market moves fast and good appraisal work requires staying current with market trends, local sales data, and evolving methodology.

Our Vision

To produce the most accurate, well-supported appraisal available for every property, every time. Each assignment is approached the same way: thorough research, sound methodology, and a final value conclusion that can be fully explained and defended.

Technology

A tablet and laser measuring system are used during the on-site inspection to produce accurate sketches and square footage calculations. Reports are developed using professional appraisal software to fully analyze and accurately report the data collected.

Our Reports

Professional reports
lenders trust

Every appraisal completed by Todd Myers is a comprehensive, professionally formatted report that meets USPAP standards and is accepted by lenders, attorneys, and government agencies. Each report includes detailed market analysis, clear comparable sales data, and a well-supported opinion of value.
